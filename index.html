Handsome Guy Called Prabowo

Prabowo Subianto Djojohadikusumo (Perfected Spelling: Prabowo Subianto Joyohadikusumo; born 17 October 1951) is an Indonesian politician, businessman, and former military officer who served as the eighth President of Indonesia. Previously, he was the 26th Minister of Defense under President Joko Widodo from 2019 to 2024. Prabowo is the third Indonesian president with a military background after Suharto and Susilo Bambang Yudhoyono, and is the oldest president to serve for the first time in Indonesian history.

Ex leader +62 Country

Joko Widodo ( Indonesian: [ dʒɔkɔ widɔdɔ ] ; born in 21 Juni 1961), better known as Jokowi is an Indonesian politician and businessman who served as the seventh President of Indonesia from 2014 to 2024. Previously, he was a member of the Indonesian Democratic Party of Struggle (PDI-P). He is the first Indonesian president who did not come from the political or military elite and became the first Indonesian president who previously served as a regional head, where he served as Governor of DKI Jakarta from 2012 to 2014 and Mayor of Surakarta City from 2005 to 2012.
